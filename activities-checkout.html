Tour Description

Discover an aquatic world that brings the marvels of underwater and tropical life at Dubai Aquarium & Underwater Zoo, one of the most iconic attractions in Dubai.

Dubai Aquarium & Underwater Zoo is one of the largest indoor aquariums in the World and it has won a Guinness World Record for the world’s “Largest Acrylic Panel”. The 10-million liter Dubai Aquarium tank is located in The Dubai Mall, the largest mall in the world, and it houses over 33,000 aquatic animals, comprising over 140 species. Over 300 Sharks and Rays live in this tank, including the largest collection of Sand Tiger Sharks in the world.

Located just above the aquarium, Underwater Zoo brings its visitors up close with penguins, piranhas, crabs, water rats, sea horses, jellyfish and plenty more.
